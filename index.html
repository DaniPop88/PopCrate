About Us
Our Story

Pop Crate by Thomas started in 2021 when long-time collector Thomas Lee realized there was no single Philippine destination for rare pop-culture treasures. What began as sourcing exclusives at local conventions quickly grew into an online store loved by collectors nationwide.

Our Mission

To unite Filipino collectors with the most sought-after toys and build a vibrant community around our shared passion.

Our Values
  • Authenticity: Every item is vetted for 100% legitimacy.
  • Quality: We pack and ship with care so your collectibles arrive in mint condition.
  • Community: More than a store—we’re a gathering place for fans to trade tips, trades, and stories.
