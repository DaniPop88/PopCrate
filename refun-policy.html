Refund & Return Policy

At Pop Crate by Thomas, we want every collector to be satisfied. Please review our refund and return policy below:

1. Returns

  • Returns are accepted only for items that are defective, damaged, or incorrect upon delivery.
  • Contact us within 7 days of receiving your item to request a return.
  • Items must be unused, in original condition and packaging.
  • Mystery Crate and surprise items are non-returnable unless damaged or incorrect.
  • Collectibles with tampered seals or opened packaging are not eligible for return.

2. Refunds

  • Once your return is received and inspected, we’ll notify you regarding approval or rejection of your refund.
  • If approved, your refund will be processed via your original payment method within 7–10 business days.
  • Shipping fees are non-refundable except for incorrect or defective items.

3. Exchanges

  • We only replace items if they are defective or damaged. If you need an exchange, email us at support@popcrate.ph.

4. How to Start a Return

  • Email support@popcrate.ph with your order number, reason for return, and photos (if applicable).
  • We will provide instructions for the return process.
